Our Love Story

How We Met

It was a rainy Tuesday at the local coffee shop when our eyes first met. Sarah was reading her favorite novel, and Michael accidentally bumped into her table while ordering his usual latte. What started as an embarrassed apology turned into a three-hour conversation about books, dreams, and everything in between.

The Proposal

Two years later, Michael surprised Sarah during their weekend hike at Sunset Point. As they reached the summit, he got down on one knee with the city lights twinkling below them. With tears of joy and a resounding "Yes!", our forever journey began.
