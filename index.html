Our Love Story

Two hearts, one beautiful journey

How We Met

Two Years Ago

It was during Diwali celebrations at a family friend's house where our paths first crossed. Yogen was helping with the rangoli while Palak was arranging the diyas. A shared laugh over a wonky lotus petal design sparked a conversation that lasted until the fireworks ended.

The Proposal

Six Months Ago

Under the golden sunset at India Gate, surrounded by the bustling energy of Delhi, Yogen got down on one knee. With the tricolor fluttering in the background and families secretly watching from afar, our forever journey began with Palak's joyful "Haan!"

Ring Ceremony

Three Months Ago

Surrounded by our loving families, we exchanged rings in a beautiful ceremony filled with traditional rituals, mehendi, and endless blessings. The happiness on our parents' faces made everything perfect.

Wedding Night Celebrations

A sacred evening of rituals, love, and togetherness

Baraat Departure

6:00 PM

The groom sets forth from Janta Bhawan accompanied by music, dance, and joyous celebrations. This procession marks the sacred beginning of the wedding night rituals.

Welcome Snacks

7:30 PM onwards

As early-arriving guests from both sides settle in, a variety of traditional snacks and refreshments will be served. This warm welcome ensures everyone is comfortably received while awaiting the main ceremonies.

Jai Mala

8:30 PM

The bride and groom exchange garlands in a sacred Jai Mala ceremony. The timing is flexible to ensure most cherished guests are present, allowing everyone to witness this auspicious moment of love and commitment.

Dinner

9:00 PM onwards

Following the Jai Mala, guests are invited to a lavish traditional dinner. This feast celebrates the joyous union and offers an opportunity for family and friends to come together in love and laughter.

Hawan Ritual (Yajna)

11:00 PM

The couple offers prayers and performs sacred rituals in the presence of Agni, the divine witness. This ancient tradition sanctifies the union and invokes blessings for prosperity, harmony, and spiritual growth.

Wedding Conclusion

1:00 AM

The sacred ceremonies conclude, leaving behind cherished memories and blessings for the couple. Guests depart with hearts full of joy and the warmth of togetherness.
