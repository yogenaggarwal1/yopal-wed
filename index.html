Our Wedding Festivities

Two days of love, laughter, and lasting memories ✨

Day 1: The Fun Begins (Nov 11, 2025)

Haldi ki Rasam 💛

3:00 PM

Get ready for a splash of yellow! Dher saara fun, music, and haldi ke rang. Let's bless the couple with all the pyaar and good vibes.
“Peela rang khushiyon ka, yaaron ke sang... lagega jab Yogen-Palak ko haldi ka rang!”

Sangeet & Sparkles ✨

7:00 PM onwards

An evening of engagement, cocktails, and dancing till you drop! 💃🕺 Apne dancing shoes taiyaar rakhein, kyunki yeh raat music, masti aur memories se bhari hogi. Let’s celebrate the ring ceremony with a bang!

Day 2: Our Forever Starts (Nov 12, 2025)

Baraat on the Move!

6:00 PM

The time has come! Get ready for dhol, dhamaka, aur dher saara dance 🕺. The groom is all set to bring his queen home. Baraat will leave with full energy!

Baraatiyon ka Swagat

7:30 PM onwards

Welcome at Green Island Resort! After all that dancing, it's time to refuel. Grab some delicious snacks and refreshing drinks 🍹, and get ready for the magical night ahead.

The 'Jai Mala' ✨

8:30 PM

Watch us exchange garlands and seal the deal with smiles and a whole lot of love ❤️. This is it—the moment we've all been waiting for. Get your cameras ready!

The Dinner 🍽️

9:00 PM onwards

Shaadi ho aur khaana na ho? Impossible! Get ready to treat your taste buds to a royal feast. Eat, drink, and be merry, because this celebration is for you! 🎉

Promises by the Fire 🔥

11:00 PM

Witness the sacred pheras as we take seven steps around the holy fire, making promises of love, respect, and endless partnership 💍. This is where our forever truly begins.

A New Beginning 🚗

1:00 AM

With happy tears 🥹 and hearts full of love, we start our new journey together. Thank you for being a part of our big day and showering us with your blessings!
