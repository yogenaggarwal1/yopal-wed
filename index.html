Our Love Story

How We Met

Two Years Ago

It was during Diwali celebrations at a family friend's house where our paths first crossed. Yogen was helping with the rangoli while Palak was arranging the diyas. A shared laugh over a wonky lotus petal design sparked a conversation that lasted until the fireworks ended.

The Proposal

Six Months Ago

Under the golden sunset at India Gate, surrounded by the bustling energy of Delhi, Yogen got down on one knee. With the tricolor fluttering in the background and families secretly watching from afar, our forever journey began with Palak's joyful "Haan!"

Ring Ceremony

Three Months Ago

Surrounded by our loving families, we exchanged rings in a beautiful ceremony filled with traditional rituals, mehendi, and endless blessings. The happiness on our parents' faces made everything perfect.
